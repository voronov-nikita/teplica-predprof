THE PERFECT GREENHOUSE

If you have a greenhouse in the country or on any other site, then the application developed by us is just for you. It will help you to work remotely with the greenhouse: find out the temperature, humidity of the air and soil at a given time, and depending on this, open and close the windows or turn on watering, or specify a certain time, and all this will happen automatically. You will also be able to view your work statistics in graphs. If you are interested in the above, click on the button on the right and download our application. For more information about the application and how to download it, see the links at the top of the site.
