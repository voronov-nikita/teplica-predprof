THE PERFECT GREENHOUSE

If you have a greenhouse in the country or on any other site, then the application developed by us is just for you. It will help you to work remotely with the greenhouse: find out the temperature, humidity of the air and soil at a given time, and depending on this, open and close the windows or turn on watering, or specify a certain time, and all this will happen automatically. You will also be able to view your work statistics in graphs. If you are interested in the above, click on the button on the right and download our application. For more information about the application and how to download it, see the links at the top of the site.

Introduction

Our application is called "GreenHouse". Its main purpose: remote management of the greenhouse. The application is made mainly in black, white tones with the addition of green and yellow colors. The interface is extremely simple and clear. But if you still have any questions about its use, then all the information below will help you.

Left Menu

As soon as you open the app, you will be taken to the home screen. But now we are not talking about it, but about the “Left Menu”, which you can enter by clicking on the icon in the upper left corner. This is the basis through which we work with the application. That is why reading about it, you will learn about all parts of the application.
The menu contains: Home, EDIT, Table, Auto Mode, Extra Mode, Themes, GitHub. Let's take a closer look at each of them.

Home

Home is the main screen on which there are 4 main buttons that show the temperature and humidity of the air for each sensor at a given time. The sait info button takes us to a similar page, but there are already 6 main buttons on it that show soil moisture, and also by pressing back, you can go back. The DO button at the bottom will take us to a page where we can open the windows, turn on and off watering, but only if all other sensor indicators do not exceed the norm or are not underestimated. The indicators at which the buttons in the DO section will not be active, you can set in the next section of the menu – EDIT.

EDIT

As mentioned earlier, this section is designed to set sensor readings, in which the buttons in the Home/DO section will not be active, and you will not be able to open watering or windows. In EDIT, in the lines Temperature, Humidity Air, Humidity Soil, you enter values and save by clicking on Save from below.

Table

Here you can find tables where all the information from the main screen is collected in a more compact form.To update the data, there is a "New data" button at the bottom.

Extra Mode

This page will allow you, despite the sensor readings, to open watering or windows, which you would not be able to do in do. Click on the walker, and the buttons become active. You should use this section only in emergency situations.

Auto Mode

By clicking on the Auto Mode button, you will go to the wonderful part of our application, where you can set a certain time when the watering or window will open automatically. This way it will be more convenient for you, and you will definitely not forget anything. Click on the walker, after which the Set Time button will become active, and you can set the time from the keyboard or by the dial. The time you set will be saved on the screen.

Themes

A feature such as changing the app theme from dark to light and back is also available. To do this, just click on “Themes".

GitHub

We left a link button to our repository on GitHub, in case someone would be interested to know how our application was created.

Assistance

In the upper right corner of the "Left menu" section is the "question" icon. She will redirect you to our website if you have any questions.

Successful use!
